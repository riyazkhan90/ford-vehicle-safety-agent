System FAQ
What is this?
A diagnostics assistant that answers questions about Ford vehicle safety complaints and recalls using live, public NHTSA data.
What can I ask?
Complaint trends by model and year ("What's the most common Explorer issue in 2022?"), open recalls for a specific model/year, or specific complaint narratives ("Any transmission reports on the Bronco?").
Which models and years are covered?
Ford Bronco, Explorer, Mustang, Escape, and Edge — model years 2020 to 2024.
Where does the data come from?
Two live sources: NHTSA's public complaints and recalls APIs (official government data, no API key needed), plus a local vector index of complaint text for semantic search.
Is this an official Ford product?
No. This is an independent portfolio project built on public government data. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by Ford Motor Company.
